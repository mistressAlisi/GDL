HOW IT WORKS

How to Earn Points

Players earn points on SportsLotto by participating in daily game-day events and selecting tickets that include real sporting events. Each ticket is built around one or more games, and points are awarded based on how those selected events perform once the games are completed.

The objective is to make accurate selections across multiple events. Players can choose smaller point amounts while selecting more games on a ticket, creating a lottery-style structure where correctly predicting several outcomes can result in higher point rewards. Tickets may include up to ten events, allowing players to balance risk and reward based on their preferences.

Points are awarded after tickets are graded using official game results. The more successful the selections on a ticket, the more points can be earned.

All participation uses points only, and no deposits or payments are required at any time.

How to Redeem Amazon Gift Cards

SportsLotto allows players to redeem earned points for real Amazon gift cards with real monetary value. Gift cards are issued electronically and delivered at no charge once redemption requirements are met.

100 Points = $1 USD

Example: 1,000 points = $10 Amazon gift card

Gift cards are provided digitally and can be used in accordance with Amazon's terms and conditions.

To be eligible for redemption, players must first participate in gameplay using at least one ticket after receiving their initial registration points.

This helps ensure fair participation and platform integrity. Once eligible, players can submit a redemption request through their account, and approved gift cards are sent electronically.

All redemptions are processed using points only. No deposits or payments are required at any time.
